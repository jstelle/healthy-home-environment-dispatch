What Are Some Natural Cleaners that You Can Use to Clean Your Home?

Natural cleaners are eco-friendly alternatives to commercial cleaning products. They are made from ingredients found in your kitchen or garden, such as lemon juice, baking soda, or even vinegar. Natural cleaners work just as well as traditional products, but they avoid the risk of harmful chemicals and also don’t pollute the environment when they are used. When shopping for natural cleaners, look for ones that are unscented, as these are less likely to irritate sensitive skin. Natural cleaners can be used for a variety of household tasks, including cleaning floors, removing stains from clothing and countertops, and sanitizing kitchen appliances.
